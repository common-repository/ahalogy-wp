Enables the automatic showcasing of your site’s content into the Ahalogy Sponsored Content Program.

== Description ==

If you are a member of the Ahalogy Sponsored Content Program, setting up this Wordpress plugin lets you showcase your content to our Brand Partners to help you achieve sponsored content deals.

Ahalogy partners with brands and publishers to create sponsored content that’s a win for everyone. Content creators get the opportunity to earn additional income doing what you already do best. Your readers get more of the unique, useful content they’re craving. And Ahalogy brand partners get the high-quality content they need to connect with their audience and achieve their marketing goals.

The plugin also continues to support our Pinterest tools, displaying audience interstitials to site traffic from Pinterest and powering Insights on Pins posted through Ahalogy.

**To participate it’s important that you first:**

1. Are a member of Ahalogy. Not a member? You can [apply to join here](https://www.ahalogy.com/publishers/?utm_source=wordpress&utm_medium=installation_instructions&utm_content=apply_to_join&utm_campaign=sponsored_content_plugin).
2. Have signed up to participate in our Sponsored Content Program. [Learn more](https://help.ahalogy.com/customer/en/portal/topics/747087-sponsored-content-program/articles?utm_source=wordpress&utm_medium=installation_instructions&utm_content=learn_more&utm_campaign=sponsored_content_plugin)
[Visit Ahalogy](https://www.ahalogy.com/publishers/?utm_source=wordpress&utm_medium=installation_instructions&utm_content=visit_ahalogy&utm_campaign=sponsored_content_plugin) for more information.

== Installation ==

**Plugin Search**

* In your Admin, go to menu Plugins > Add.
* Search for Ahalogy.
* Find the plugin that’s labeled {Ahalogy Sponsored Content}.
* Look for the author name Ahalogy on the plugin.
* Click to install.
* Activate the plugin.
* A new menu item *Ahalogy* will appear under your Settings menu option.

**Download & Upload**

* Download the plugin (a zip file) on the right column of this page.
* In your Admin, go to menu Plugins > Add.
* Select the tab *Upload*.
* Upload the .zip file you just downloaded.
* Activate the plugin.
* A new menu item *Ahalogy* will appear under your Settings menu option.

**FTP Upload**

* Download the plugin (.zip file) on the right column of this page.
* Unzip the zip file contents.
* Upload the Ahalogy folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory of your site.
* Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress.
* A new menu item *Ahalogy* will appear under your Settings menu option.
